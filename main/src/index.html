The Angsty Feminists in Tech Initiative

The Angsty Feminists in Tech Initiative is a web project made for and by intersectional feminists in tech. We celebrate and recognise that we are standing on the shoulders of giants: the feminists that came before us whose work led to the rights for women to work, vote, and have a voice in our society.

We do not tolerate prejudice, discrimination, or hate speech that is sexist, racist, classist, ableist, homo/bi/transphobic, or discriminatory based on religion. Rather, we champion active inclusion of our most vulnerable and oppressed communities.
